Vinted 营收暴涨38%，二手时尚市场结构性崛起

Second-Hand Fashion Platform Vinted Reports 38% Jump in Revenue

Business of Fashion / FashionNetwork Business of Fashion · 2026-04-09

要点：欧洲二手时尚平台 Vinted 营收同比大涨38%，贝恩数据显示二手奢侈品市场2030年将达3200-3600亿美元。贝莱德入股 Vinted，估值达70亿英镑。Gen Z 超过55%的二手购买发生在 Vestiaire Collective、Vinted 等专业平台。

Key Points: European second-hand platform Vinted reported 38% YoY revenue growth. Bain data shows the second-hand luxury market will reach $320-360 billion by 2030. BlackRock invested in Vinted at a £7 billion valuation. Gen Z makes over 55% of resale purchases through specialist platforms like Vestiaire Collective and Vinted.

洞察：二手时尚已从边缘习惯跃升为主流趋势，这不是消费降级，而是年轻人重新定义奢侈品价值的文化宣言。品牌方需将二手市场纳入生态体系而非视为威胁，否则将在Z世代争夺战中逐渐失语。

Insight: Second-hand fashion has evolved from a fringe habit into a mainstream trend — not downtrading, but a cultural manifesto redefining luxury value. Brands must integrate resale into their ecosystem rather than treating it as a threat, or risk losing relevance in the Gen Z market.

质感当道：Highsnobiety 定义 2026 秋冬核心设计语言

On Fashion's Tactility Takeover: Texture Is the New Black

Highsnobiety Highsnobiety · 2026-03-19

要点：Highsnobiety 与 Mr Porter 联合发布的2026秋冬趋势报告显示，质感纹理（Texture）取代色彩成为最核心设计语言。从 Acne Studios 的棉质皮革拼接夹克到 Jil Sander 填充丝巾，材质本身的触感与视觉双重属性被前所未有地放大。

Key Points: A joint Highsnobiety and Mr Porter trend report identifies tactile texture as the dominant design language for Fall/Winter 2026, replacing color. From Acne Studios' cotton-leather trimmed jacket to Jil Sander's padded silk-blend hoodie, material properties — both tactile and visual — are amplified to an unprecedented degree.

洞察：质感复兴背后是消费者对数字时代"轻盈感"的集体反叛。当一切可被屏幕复制，真实可触的材质反而成为最难被取代的奢侈品核心属性。这与"努力美学"深度共鸣，共同指向时尚向手工与真实性的价值回归。

Insight: The texture renaissance reflects collective rebellion against digital-age "weightlessness." Tangible materiality becomes the last irreplaceable luxury core when everything can be replicated on screen. This resonates deeply with the "Try-Hard Era" — together pointing to fashion's return to craftsmanship and authenticity.

"努力美学"时代：时尚进入高难度纪元

Fashion Is in Its Try-Hard Era: Effort Becomes Desirable Again

Highsnobiety Highsnobiety · 2026-02-10

要点：Highsnobiety 文化评论深度剖析"努力美学"（Try-Hard Era）现象。Bottega Veneta 的"Craft Is Our Language" campaign、Prada 2026秋冬男装系列的污渍袖口与龟裂风衣、多品牌对文学世界的集体拥抱，共同勾勒出：努力、摩擦、手工痕迹正在成为新的奢侈品美学语言。

Key Points: Highsnobiety's deep cultural analysis identifies the "Try-Hard Era" phenomenon. Bottega Veneta's "Craft Is Our Language" campaign, Prada FW26's dirtied cuffs and cracking raincoats, and luxury houses' collective embrace of literary aesthetics all point to effort, friction, and visible craftsmanship as the new language of luxury.

洞察："努力美学"是对数字时代"轻松文化"的深度解毒。算法时代内容消费极度被动，而"需要付出努力才能理解"的时尚，因稀缺性和人与人之间的真实连接而变得珍贵。品牌应将工艺叙事与过程透明化作为后疫情时代品牌建设核心策略。

Insight: The "Try-Hard Era" is a radical antidote to digital-age "effortless culture." Fashion that demands effort to appreciate becomes precious through its rarity and authentic human connection. Brands should make craftsmanship narratives and process transparency central to post-pandemic brand-building strategy.
